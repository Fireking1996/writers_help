    Publishing Options

    Not sure whether to self-publish or pursue traditional publishing? Use this checklist to explore your path.

    Self-Publishing: Is it for you?

    • You want full creative control over your book
    • You’re willing to handle or hire for editing, cover design, and marketing
    • You want faster publication (days/weeks, not months/years)
    • You’re comfortable managing or learning publishing platforms (e.g., Amazon KDP)

    Traditional Publishing: Is it your goal?

    • You want access to professional editors, designers, and marketers
    • You’re willing to wait and submit to agents or publishers
    • You prefer a structured publishing timeline and wide bookstore access
    • You’re prepared for potential rejection and revision cycles
